TimeZ

Time at your fingertips

Features

World Time with day and night landscapes

Add cities from different countries and time zones and know the exact time.

A minimal Day and Night background tells you if it's a day or night in the city so that you can plan your calls/meetings accordingly.

Time at your fingertips

Change the current time using the sliders and know the respective time in your favourite cities. TimeZ does the time difference calculations for you.

Easily switch between the 12-Hour and 24-Hour time format using the time format switch control.

Over 7000+ cities supported

Choose and add your favourite cities where your loved ones, your clients, or your employees are.

Quickly find the city you are looking for by using the search option. You can search by city, time zone, province or country.

Reorder cities as per your preference.

Wish to see the cities you added in your custom sequence? You can do that by simple Drag and Drop.

On iPhone or iPad, long-press on a city and drag to reorder. On Mac, simply drag a city to reorder.

Supports dark mode.

Wish to see the app in dark mode or light mode? The app supports dark and light modes and automatically changes with the system settings.

Widgets

Quckly have a glance at the time of your favourite cities without opening the app.
